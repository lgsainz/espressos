Events


Live music, poetry readings, book signings, and much more are featured at Espressos. Visit the shop and view the community board for any upcoming events. The restaurant is available for private parties as well.

Music details: Features some great local artists, musicians, and poets. It is an "open mic" and highlights some great artists. We already have our "regular" musicians who show up just about every Thursday, however any musicians or poets that are interested in performing, we ask you show up early to introduce yourself to the host of the event.

Food: The food menu is a carry on from the morning and lunch menu. You can order from over 10 sandwich options, soups, salads, as well as the full breakfast menu! Waffles with fresh fruit, omeletes, etc. Soups are made from scratch, salads are mouthwatering and of course, fresh made desserts, such as canoli's, seasonal desserts, and more!
