About Us...

It's simple: Provide customers with a relaxing environment, and excellent staff serving top notch specialty drinks and a full food menu! Start off with the finest coffees from around the globe, add Italian chocolates and caramels, throw in the finest French Monin syrups, add homemade desserts we baked onsite as well as from local bakeries and lastly, serve a FULL BREAKFAST AND LUNCH menu that is available 7 DAYS A WEEK! We also offer a lite fair evening menu.

We don't even have a freezer because we believe that fresh tastes best!
